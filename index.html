How it works

Post your Freight in minutes, receive bids or choose instant match, and track delivery with escrow protection.

Post your Freight

Enter pickup & drop-off, weight/units, choose delivery type, then pick a date and 1-hour time window. You can attach images or PDFs if needed.

Drivers bid or instant match

Compare bids by price, ETA, and rating — or select instant match at a system price for guaranteed booking.

Pay via escrow & track

Funds are held until delivery is confirmed. Time-slot rules apply: customer delays may add fees; late drivers are penalized.

Rate & build reputation

Both sides rate each other. High-rated drivers get priority access to jobs, and repeat customers earn perks.
